Plutz aim at comparing various data about the planets in the solar system in order to rediscover and visualize numbers that can be difficult to grasp.

How does it
work?

When you arrive in front of the solar system, all planets can be visualized. You can navigate through the different data by clicking on the corresponding icons and compare these data to each other. In order to visualize the data about a specific planet, just scroll until you reach the planet you are looking for.
